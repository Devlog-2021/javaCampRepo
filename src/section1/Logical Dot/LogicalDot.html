Logical Dot


You are going to draw a circle that changes color when you click the mouse.

1. Create a new sketch in Processing and add a setup() method.

2. Set the size of your sketch in your setup() method to 600, 600.

3. Add a draw() method to your sketch.

4. Draw an ellipse in your draw() method.

5. Make your ellipse a nice color.

TESTING
